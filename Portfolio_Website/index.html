ABOUT

Hi, I'm Kathleen, a UX Designer living in San Francisco. I love figuring out how things work and what makes people tick. I dig getting to the heart of problems and developing solutions that solve problems for people and businesses. I approach design with a systems perspective informed by my years of experience as a mechanical system designer. I'm an engineer at heart and am driven to figure out how things work to understand the possibilities and contraints of problems.

I designed Heating, Ventilation and Air Conditioning (HVAC) systems primarily in data centers, commercial buildings and schools. This entailed balancing the requirements of users and stakeholders, building codes and construction methods while meeting energy use goals and keeping on budget. I had a broad range of responsiblities in taking projects from conceptual design to commissioning & occupancy including:

  • Definition of initial project goals and requirements and their reevaluation throughout design.
  • Energy modelling from conceptual design to completion to help architects define building façade and meet energy goals.
  • Calculation of heating and cooling loads based on building geometry, occupants, weather and internal loads to size mechanical equipment.
  • Iterative development of 2D/3D models of HVAC systems to coordinate with other systems & coordination with other trades to integrate systems.
  • Development of construction drawings & specifications and coordination with construction team to verify requirements met.
  • Writing functional performance tests to verify systems are operating per specified sequence of operations.
  • Performing trend reviews of control system data to troubleshoot performance issues and/or identify controls modifications to save energy.
