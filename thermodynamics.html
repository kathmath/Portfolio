LAWS OF THERMODYNAMICS

CSS ANIMATIONS

One day I was trying to think of the laws of thermodynamics and realized I couldn't remember which was which. Having studied mechanical engineering this was somewhat distressing. I looked them up and got to thinking this was a good opportunity to play with CSS animations and try to figure out how to present abstract concepts visually. They're in progress and I expect it'll be something I'm forever trying to get right but it has been a fun exercize.

See the Pen Zeroth Law of Thermodynamics by Kathleen Matthews (@kathmath) on CodePen.
